I am Roselida Akoth Ochieng'

A passionate frontend developer, who loves bringing life to websites, I come from Kenya. I am skilled in React.js, Javascript, HTML, CSS, Sass, Bootstrap and headless content Management Systems such as Storyblok.

I am involved in unblocking students on their frontend development projects and participating in student study sessions. I love learning new technologies, taking a keen look at what problems they are solving and how can I use them to build better and scalable products.
